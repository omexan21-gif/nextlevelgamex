About Us

Next Level Game Exchange is Saskatoon's go-to destination for new and used video games, consoles, and accessories. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, or trade your games, or need a fast professional repair, we've got you covered.

Our inventory is updated in real-time and we carry hundreds of titles across all major platforms — from retro classics to the latest releases. Our repair techs handle everything from HDMI ports to screen replacements with a fast 24–48 hour turnaround.
